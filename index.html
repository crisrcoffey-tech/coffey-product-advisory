How it works

A practical engagement model designed to produce usable outputs quickly.

01

Assess the current operating system

Review product process, release flow, tooling, metrics, Jira/GitHub usage, documentation, QA, and team bottlenecks.

02

Identify high-value AI workflow opportunities

Prioritize places where AI can reduce manual effort, improve signal quality, or accelerate delivery.

03

Build working workflows

Create practical automations, dashboards, templates, prompts, and team playbooks that can be used immediately.

04

Train the team and stabilize adoption

Document the process, train stakeholders, refine based on usage, and leave the company with repeatable systems.
